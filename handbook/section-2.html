Section 2 – Definitions of Employee Status

SymTec Employee Handbook

The following definitions are used throughout this Handbook to describe employee classification and eligibility.

2.1 Full-Time Employees

Full-time employees are those who are regularly scheduled to work at least thirty (30) hours per week. Full-time employees are eligible for benefits as described in this Handbook.

2.2 Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees are those who are regularly scheduled to work fewer than thirty (30) hours per week. Part-time employees may be eligible for certain benefits as determined by the Company.
