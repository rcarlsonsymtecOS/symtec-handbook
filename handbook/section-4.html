Section 4 – Employment Policies

SymTec Employee Handbook

4.1 Equal Employment Opportunity

SymTec is an equal opportunity employer. Employment decisions are made without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, veteran status, or any other status protected by applicable law.

4.2 Anti-Harassment Policy

SymTec is committed to providing a work environment free from harassment. Harassment of any kind is prohibited and will not be tolerated. Employees who experience or witness harassment should report it to their Team Lead or management promptly.

4.3 Non-Discrimination

Discrimination in employment practices is strictly prohibited. This includes hiring, promotion, compensation, training, discipline, and termination decisions.

4.4 Employment Eligibility

All employees must be legally authorized to work in the United States. Employment eligibility verification is required in accordance with applicable federal law.

4.5 Employment Classifications

Employee classifications are defined in Section 2 of this Handbook. Questions regarding classification or eligibility should be directed to your Team Lead.

SymTec reserves the right to interpret and apply these employment policies as necessary to comply with applicable laws and business needs.
