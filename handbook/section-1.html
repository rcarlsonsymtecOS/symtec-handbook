Section 1 – Introduction

SymTec Employee Handbook

1.1 Welcome

Welcome to SymTec. This Employee Handbook has been prepared to provide employees with information about company policies, procedures, and expectations.

1.2 Purpose of the Handbook

This Handbook is designed to acquaint employees with SymTec and to provide information about working conditions, benefits, and policies affecting employment.

1.3 Employment At-Will

Employment with SymTec is voluntarily entered into, and employees are free to resign at any time, for any reason or no reason. Similarly, SymTec may terminate employment at any time, with or without cause or notice.

1.4 Accuracy of Information

SymTec relies upon the accuracy of information contained in the employment application and other data presented throughout the hiring process and employment. Any misrepresentations, falsifications, or material omissions may result in termination of employment.
