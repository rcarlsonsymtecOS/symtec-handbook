Section 7 – Benefits & Services

SymTec Employee Handbook

7.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)

SymTec provides Paid Time Off (PTO) to eligible employees to allow time away from work for rest, personal needs, and other approved purposes. PTO accrual, usage, and approval requirements are determined by company policy and communicated to employees.

7.2 Holidays

SymTec observes designated company holidays each year. A list of recognized holidays will be communicated annually. Employees may be eligible for holiday pay based on their employment classification.

7.3 Leave of Absence

Leaves of absence may be available in accordance with applicable law and company policy. Requests for leave should be submitted in advance whenever possible and require approval.

7.4 Employee Assistance and Resources

SymTec may provide access to employee assistance resources and services designed to support employee well-being and work-life balance.

7.5 Training and Development

SymTec supports ongoing training and professional development. Participation in training programs may require approval from a Team Lead and must align with business needs.

Employees with questions regarding benefits or services should contact their Team Lead for additional information.
