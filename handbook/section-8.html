Section 8 – Employee Communications

SymTec Employee Handbook

8.1 Open Door Policy

SymTec encourages open and honest communication. Employees are encouraged to raise questions, concerns, or suggestions with their Team Lead or management at any time.

8.2 Problem Resolution

Employees should first attempt to resolve workplace issues at the lowest possible level by discussing concerns directly with their Team Lead. If additional assistance is needed, concerns may be escalated to management as appropriate.

8.3 Policy Questions

Questions regarding the interpretation or application of policies in this Handbook should be directed to a Team Lead. The Company reserves the right to interpret and apply policies as necessary.

8.4 Handbook Acknowledgment

Employees are responsible for reading and understanding the policies contained in this Handbook. Continued employment constitutes acknowledgment of responsibility to comply with these policies.

If a topic is not addressed in this Handbook, employees should consult their Team Lead for guidance.
