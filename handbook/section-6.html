Section 6 – Compensation & Benefits

SymTec Employee Handbook

6.1 Compensation

Employees are compensated based on their role, responsibilities, experience, and performance. Compensation practices are designed to be competitive and equitable while supporting business objectives.

6.2 Payroll Practices

Employees are paid in accordance with established payroll schedules. Payroll deductions required by law or authorized by the employee will be made as applicable.

6.3 Overtime

Non-exempt employees may be eligible for overtime compensation in accordance with applicable wage and hour laws. Overtime must be approved in advance by a Team Lead.

6.4 Benefits Eligibility

Eligibility for benefits is determined by employment classification and length of service. Details regarding specific benefits are outlined in applicable benefit plan documents.

6.5 Benefit Changes

SymTec reserves the right to modify, amend, or terminate any benefit plan at any time, subject to applicable law. Employees will be notified of material changes.

Employees with questions regarding compensation or benefits should contact their Team Lead for clarification.
