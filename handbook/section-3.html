Section 3 – Mission and Values

SymTec Employee Handbook

3.1 Mission Statement

SymTec’s mission is to provide reliable, secure, and effective IT solutions that make work easier, businesses stronger, and lives better.

3.2 Core Values

The following core values guide SymTec’s decisions, behavior, and culture. All employees are expected to uphold these values in their daily work.

  • Integrity: Acting honestly, ethically, and responsibly in all situations.
  • Service: Delivering exceptional service to clients and colleagues.
  • Teamwork: Working collaboratively and supporting one another.
  • Growth: Pursuing continuous improvement and professional development.
  • Accountability: Taking ownership of actions, results, and commitments.

3.3 Workplace Expectations

Employees are expected to conduct themselves in a manner consistent with SymTec’s mission and values at all times. Failure to adhere to these values may result in corrective action.

If employees have questions regarding company values or expectations, they should consult their Team Lead for guidance.
