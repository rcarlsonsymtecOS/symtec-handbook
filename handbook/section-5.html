Section 5 – Standards of Conduct

SymTec Employee Handbook

SymTec expects all employees to conduct themselves in a professional, ethical, and respectful manner at all times. These standards apply to behavior toward clients, coworkers, vendors, and the public.

5.1 Professional Conduct

Employees are expected to act with honesty, integrity, and professionalism in all work-related activities. Conduct that negatively affects the company’s reputation or operations may result in corrective action.

5.2 Attendance and Punctuality

Regular attendance and punctuality are essential to business operations. Employees are expected to report to work as scheduled and to notify their Team Lead as soon as possible if they will be late or absent.

5.3 Workplace Behavior

Inappropriate behavior, including abusive language, threats, violence, or disruptive conduct, will not be tolerated. SymTec is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful workplace.

5.4 Use of Company Property

Company property, including equipment, systems, and resources, must be used responsibly and primarily for business purposes. Unauthorized or improper use may result in disciplinary action.

5.5 Conflicts of Interest

Employees must avoid situations in which personal interests conflict, or appear to conflict, with the interests of SymTec. Any potential conflicts of interest should be disclosed to management.

Violations of these standards may result in corrective or disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
